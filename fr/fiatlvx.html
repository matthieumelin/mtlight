Fiat LVX

A painter since the late 1990s, Zoltan first navigated between graffiti, mural and oil painting. Also fascinated by the work of the great Spanish masters, he completed a bachelor’s degree in painting and drawing at Concordia (Montreal) in 2012, then a master’s degree at the University of Ottawa in 2018. During his studies , he explores the evolution of different pictorial techniques and deconstructs the material image to graft the synthetic image onto it.
